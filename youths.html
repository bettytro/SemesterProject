Youths

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science?

Come on down to our museum, there's plenty to see and do. We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you'll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

Wanna travel in time?

You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth? Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore.

Did you know?

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. Do your own experiments and learn cool tricks that will blow your friends mind.

Do you know which animal this is?

Did you know that the mammoth lived about 4000 years ago? Come to us and learn about how they lived and what they eat. We have a whole section dedicated to the life long before your great great grandparents lived.
